The COTIDIANA system will enable holistic and efficient patient monitoring for clinical care, clinical research, and drug trials.

Objetive monitoring of the rheumatic condition using passive sensing


Hand dexterity

Monitors speed and errors while typing on the smartphone keyboard, to detect impacts of condition on the hand joints.

Mobility

Uses the smartphone accelerometer to track gait and physical activity, providing data about mobility, fatigue, or pain.

Mental health

Tracks number of calls, SMS, and social media use, to track depression aggravation.

Mobile Patient-Reported Outcome Measures

COTIDIANA offers personalised Patient-Reported Outcome Measures, optimised for the smartphone screen, and validated in usability tests with older adult patients.

Patient monitoring at a distance


Dedicated platform for clinicians and clinical researchers that enables:
  • To monitor patient symptoms;
  • Personalize patient reported outcomes;
  • Prepare electronic case report form, for clinical research or drug trials
